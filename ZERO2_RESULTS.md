## Tuned Utilization Launchers

- The DDP baseline came from [run_stage2_ddp.sh](/l/users/youssef.ghallab/ml710/scripts/run_stage2_ddp.sh): `per_device_train_batch_size=16`, `gradient_accumulation_steps=1`, `learning_rate=2e-4`, `warmup_ratio=0.03`, `lr_scheduler_type=cosine`, `optim=adamw_torch`, `num_train_epochs=1`, with `--ddp_find_unused_parameters False`.
- The tuned ZeRO-2 launcher is [run_stage2_zero2_lora_util.sh](/l/users/youssef.ghallab/ml710/scripts/run_stage2_zero2_lora_util.sh). It raises `per_device_train_batch_size` from `1` to `2`, cuts `gradient_accumulation_steps` from `8` to `4` to keep effective batch `16`, sets `DATALOADER_WORKERS=4`, lowers logging overhead with `LOGGING_STEPS=5`, and keeps the current Vicuna recipe: `learning_rate=2e-4`, `mm_projector_lr=2e-5`, `warmup_ratio=0.03`, `lr_scheduler_type=cosine`, `optim=adamw_torch`.
- The tuned ZeRO-3 launcher is [run_stage2_zero3_lora_util.sh](/l/users/youssef.ghallab/ml710/scripts/run_stage2_zero3_lora_util.sh). It uses `per_device_train_batch_size=2`, `gradient_accumulation_steps=2` for effective batch `8`, `DATALOADER_WORKERS=4`, `LOGGING_STEPS=5`, and the Vicuna ZeRO-3 defaults `learning_rate=5e-5`, `mm_projector_lr=2e-5`, `warmup_ratio=0.1`, `lr_scheduler_type=cosine`, `optim=adamw_torch`.
- The ZeRO-3 launcher points at [zero3_bounded_async.json](/l/users/youssef.ghallab/ml710/configs/zero3_bounded_async.json), which keeps ZeRO stage `3`, enables overlapped communication, and bounds the async communication buckets with `allgather_bucket_size`, `reduce_bucket_size`, `sub_group_size`, and `stage3_prefetch_bucket_size` all set to `50000000`.
- Rationale for both utilization launchers: larger microbatches, fewer accumulation steps at the same effective batch, more dataloader workers, and lower trainer-side logging overhead. For ZeRO-3, the bounded async communication settings are intended to keep overlap enabled without leaving bucket sizing fully unconstrained.
